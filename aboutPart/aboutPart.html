About

Who are we?
We are a restaurant that aims to offer a contemporary and different culinary experience. Our dishes, prepared with fresh, high-quality products, combine the best examples of both local and international products. Our chefs prepare each dish with great care to make it taste good, not only tastefully, but also visually. Our main goal is to ensure that each of our guests enjoys their meals in a special and comfortable environment.
More About Us
Our restaurant was established with the aim of creating a unique place for modern food enthusiasts in the year 2023. Our philosophy is simple: to leave a mark on everyone with fresh products, creative recipes and superior customer service.
Our mission:
To offer our customers not just a meal, but also a special experience. To create healthy and delicious choices by using high quality, natural and local products in our meals. To further enrich the Matbex civilization and to enable our guests to discover a new world of taste. Our vision: We want to see our restaurant as not just a place to eat, but a lovely place for families and friends to gather. Each of our tables is a center for both relaxing and creating unforgettable memories.
Our specialties:
Local and International Flavors: In our menu, you can taste the traditional tastes of local cuisine as well as modern flavors of world cuisine. Unikal Space Design: Our beautifully designed interior offers a comfortable and warm atmosphere to every visitor. Customer Satisfaction: Our Peşəkar command is always here to satisfy our customers by paying maximum attention to their needs.
Our services:
Special menu and venue guarantee for individual measures and special days. Online ordering and fast delivery service. Our future depends on the satisfaction of our customers and this is our greatest motivation. Every mansion is like a family to us, and we are here to welcome this family!
